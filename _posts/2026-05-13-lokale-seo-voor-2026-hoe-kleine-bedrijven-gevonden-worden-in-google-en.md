If you run a local business, local SEO is the most important marketing you can do. People search for "plumber near me" or "hairdresser Amsterdam." If you're not in the top 3, you're missing customers.

## What is local SEO?

Local SEO optimizes your online presence to be found in local searches. Three pillars:

1. Google Business Profile
2. Reviews
3. Local content

## Google Business Profile

This is the map listing that appears in Google. The most important factor for local SEO.

## Conclusion

Local SEO isn't difficult. Start with a complete Google Business Profile, collect reviews, and write pages for local search terms.
