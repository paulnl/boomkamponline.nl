Many businesses spend hours every week on tasks a computer could handle.
Forwarding form submissions, sending emails after a purchase, copying data 
from one place to another. n8n automates this kind of work — without 
requiring any programming.

## What exactly is n8n?

n8n (pronounced "n-eight-n") is a workflow automation tool. You build 
workflows with it: when this happens, do that. For example:

- When someone submits a contact form → send an email + save to a spreadsheet
- When a new invoice comes in → check the amount and send a notification
- Every day at 8:00 AM → fetch the latest orders and compile a report

The main difference from tools like Zapier or Make: n8n is self-hosted. 
Your data stays on your own server, not with a third party.

## What does it cost?

n8n has a free open-source version that you can run on a VPS. 
A small server costs €5-10 per month. That's enough for most small businesses.

There's also a cloud version, but for privacy-sensitive applications I 
recommend the self-hosted option.

## When does it make sense?

n8n is useful if you recognize at least one of these:

- You send the same emails manually every week
- You copy data from one system to another
- You sometimes forget to respond to inquiries on time
- You work with multiple tools that don't talk to each other

## When is it NOT useful?

If you only need to do something once or twice, automation takes more 
effort than it saves. n8n is most valuable for tasks you repeat weekly 
or daily.

## How to get started

The easiest way: have someone build your first workflow for you. 
That way you'll see what's possible and what it delivers, without 
spending weeks figuring it out yourself.
