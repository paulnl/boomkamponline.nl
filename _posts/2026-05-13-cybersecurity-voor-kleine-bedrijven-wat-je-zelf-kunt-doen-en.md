Cybersecurity sounds like something for big companies with IT departments. But did you know that nearly half of all cyberattacks target small businesses? And that an average hack costs a small business tens of thousands of euros?

## What you can do yourself

1. Use a password manager
2. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
3. Keep your software up to date
4. Make weekly backups
5. Train your employees

Cybersecurity isn't difficult or expensive. The basics can be set up in an afternoon.
